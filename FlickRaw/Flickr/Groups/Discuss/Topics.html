    Post a new discussion topic to a group.

    Arguments

    group_id (required)

    The NSID of the group to add a topic to.

    subject (required)

    The topic subject.

    message (required)

    The topic message.

    Error codes

      The group by that ID does not exist

      The post message is too long.

      Subject and message are required.
